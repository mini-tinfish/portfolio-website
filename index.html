who

I'm an Information Technology student at Augusta University passionate about building secure, user-centered systems that blend creativity with strong technical foundations. I love working at the intersection of design, functionality, and cybersecurity.

I'm pursuing a B.S. in IT with a minor in Business Administration, working toward an MS in ISM and, ultimately, a PhD in Cybersecurity.

Outside of tech: bookworm, flute player, Animal Crossing fisher and bug catcher.

why mini-tinfish?

The name goes back years — a mashup of "mini" (small, unassuming) and "tinfish" (old naval slang for a torpedo). There's something fitting about that for someone going into cybersecurity: under the radar, but capable of making an impact. It stuck, and now it's the username you'll find me under across most of my tech spaces.
